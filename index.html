The Studio

Built behind
a camera.

Aureus Collections is the studio of Luke Appleby, a filmmaker who gives a fifteen-second ad the same obsession as a wedding film. Lighting, framing, pacing. Nothing on screen by accident.

The tools are simple: a Sony FX3, good glass, and the patience to wait for the right light. Luke shoots across California's Central Valley, splitting his time between brands that need video to bring in business and couples who want to remember exactly how the day felt. Different jobs, same rule. If a shot isn't worth watching twice, it never leaves the studio.
